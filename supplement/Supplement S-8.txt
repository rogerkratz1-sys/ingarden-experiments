Methods
Type I Error Diagnostic
Peripheral selection: points with Euclidean radius ? the 95th percentile of radii.
Clustering: DBSCAN on the peripheral subset with eps = 0.5, min_samples = 5. Each nonnoise DBSCAN label defines a candidate motif.
Test statistic: cluster density (T_k = n_k / A_k), where (n_k) is cluster size and (A_k) is cluster area (see S8_methods_equations.txt).
Null replicates: radialpreserving nulls (--null_method radial_preserve) with (B = 1000) MonteCarlo replicates.
Multiple testing: Benjamini¢Hochberg FDR at (\alpha = 0.05).
Reproducible command: see S8_run_commands.txt.

Label Robustness Sweep
Goal: quantify how primary motif labels change when numeric thresholds and precedence rules vary.
Twostage design:
* Stage 1 (OAT): oneatatime variations of each numeric threshold to identify sensitive keys.
* Stage 2 (Focused grid): Cartesian product over the top three sensitive keys (disp_kappa_scaled, max_block_move, order_dual_proximity) across multipliers ([0.5, 0.75, 0.9, 1.0, 1.1, 1.25, 1.5]).
Settings: 43 OAT settings + 343 focused settings = 386 total settings.
Data: 5,000 permutations ū 386 settings ? stability_map.csv.
Primary label selection: compute motif flags per permutation per setting; choose primary label by precedence order (default: ["BRE","FM/EM","APD","DCO","GD","Other"]).
Stability score: fraction of settings where the modal primary label occurs.
Precedence ensemble: 50 random precedence orders to assess tiebreaking sensitivity.
